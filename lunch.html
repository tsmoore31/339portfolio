LUNCH IDEAS

Lunch is one of my favorite meals! I often find myself eating leftovers (I'm a huge leftover advocate!!), or some fun quick meals like what you will see below! One of the better things about COVID-19 and online classes is being able to make myself a good, yummy lunch!

Salad

For the longest time, I was not a fan of salads... this summer I really learned how to make a good salad using the right ingredients and dressings. I realized that I LOVE having a mix of cold and warm vegetables, a good protein, and some fun extras! I usually start with iceberg lettuce, add in some sauteed spinach or kale, roasted vegetables (cauliflower, broccoli, zuchinni), sauteed vegetables (mushrooms, ), a good protein (tofu, vegan chicken, seitan, or beans) and some fats (avocado). I love tossing my veggies and protein in different sauces (bbq, tomato, etc.) and then adding a good dressing!

ChickPea 'Tuna' Salad Sandwich

This has been a new favorite of mine! I was never the biggest fan of tuna salad growing up, but this alternative using chickpeas instead of tuna is delicious! I mash chickpeas and mix them with vegan mayo (you can also use avocado if you want it a little healthier), celery, onion, salt and pepper! You can also add some chopped up nori sheets for a more "fishy" flavor! I love this cold in a sandwich, as a "tuna" melt, or just by itself!

Burrito

Burritos are another great lunch meal! I love them because you can also take them on the go. They are also very versatile when it comes to fillings. I love adding a protein (usually tofu or beans!), vegetables (avocado, tomato, peppers), and salsa!
