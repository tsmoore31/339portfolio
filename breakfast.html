BREAKFAST IDEAS

Breakfast is the most imporant meal of the day... yet, it is also the meal I struggle with the most. I find that I'm not super hungry in the mornings. When my classes were in person, (pre-COVID-19), I also found it difficult to make a breakfast when I had class early in the morning. Most of the time, I crave savory breakfasts! Below are some of my favorite ways to start my day!

Avocado Toast

This loaded avocado toast is my favorite way to start my morning. I love this meal because it is full of carbs, fats and proteins!

  • Mash half an avocado with lemon or lime juice and spread on a piece of toast
  • Top with tofu scramble; sautee tofu in tomato sauce and seasonings, mix in veggies (mushrooms and spinach are my favorite!)

Tofu Scramble

A tofu scramble is another great way to start the morning, especially if I don't have an avocado or bread on hand! I either prepare it with tomato sauce, as mentioned in the avo toast above, or I mix nutritional yeast, Chickenless Seasoning from Trader Joe's, Tumeric (for the yellow, egg like color!) and some other spices for flavor. I also love adding some vegetables to this!! It is a great way to get in a power punch of protein!

Smoothie

I don't often find myself craving something sweet in the morning, but when I do, I often go for a smoothie! If I work out in the morning, this is definitely something I reach for as I like adding protein powder! I like to start with a base of bananas and spinach, and I mix in ingredients such asprotein powder, frozen fruits, chia seeds, hemp seeds, peanut butter and ginger! It varies based on what I have at home, and what flavors I'm craving! I also love topping it with granola for a nice crunch!
