DINNER IDEAS

I love dinner. There is nothing more relaxing for me, than just getting to take the time to make myself a delicious meal! This is where I usually have the most time to prep a meal, so these are definitely a little more time intensive than my breakfast and lunch meals.

Pasta

Pasta is one of my favorite things to eat for dinner... there are just so many varieties! Some of my favorites are: Pesto Pasta, Vegan Pasta Bolognese, Hummus Pasta (Great for when you are out of tomato sauce!)

Lasagna

This lasagna was too good to be roped into the general pasta category!! I love dishes like this because they have a great mix of protein, carbs and fat, and also can last me a week! I made this lasagna pictured a few weeks ago and was able to have that as my main meal for awhile which proved to be very cost effective as well! The ingredients I used were: Tomato Sauce with lentils (bolognese style, lots of protein!), Spinach, Vegan Mozzerella Cheese, Vegan Ricotta Cheese (Tofu, Nutritional Yeast, Garlic, Onion, Lemon Juice, Olive Oil, Salt and Pepper!) Feel free to add in your own favorite veggies and make fun combinations! I've also made this with eggplant rather than lasagna noodles and it was delicious!

Lentil Soup

My mom is from Turkey and her Lentil Soup is one of my favorite things to have for dinner! Especially when it gets colder here in Michigan, there is nothing like warming myself up with some soup that reminds me of home. I love being able to connect with my heritage through food, and learn to cook Turkish food from my mom.

Stir Fry

Stir fries are some of my favorite dinners! I love using noodles or making more of a fried rice. I have perfected my crispy tofu recipe and it goes perfectly with these dishes. I have also started adding in vegan egg into my fried rice when I have it on hand which reminds me of when I was younger! Stir fries are a great way to get in tons of veggies and protein!
