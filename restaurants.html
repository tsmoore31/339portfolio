RESTAURANTS

Eating vegan or plant based at restaurants can definitely be tricky at first. I've learned to check menus before I go places, look up reviews, or call ahead to see if there are options! I've found that the majority of the time, restuaurants are incredibly accomodating! I've also learned to get creative when eating at restaurants, occasionally making meals out of sides! Below are some of my favorite restaurants, and tips for eating vegan at restaurants!

The Lunch Room

The Lunch Room/Detroit Filling Station is located here in Ann Arbor, Michigan and is one of my favorites! Some of my favorite dishes are:

  • Taco Salad
  • Mac and Cheese
  • Tofu Bahn Mi
  • Chkn Nuggets
